Our Purpose

At ECHO, we believe in giving every family a pathway to succeed.

That’s why we’ve designed a digital interface that brings together information on neighborhoods, public transportation, and housing unit availability to make the housing search process as easy as possible for low-income families looking to find housing in the neighborhood that’s right for them.

How it Works

Neighborhood Search

ECHO provides customized neighborhood recommendations based on the preferences and particular transit needs of families.

Look for Apartments

Listings are populated from popular real estate websites and when clicked on, show the price, address, travel details, and more.

Customize Your Results

Users enter their information into the profile page such as their budget, frequent locations, typical transit methods, and neighborhood preferences to ensure their neighborhood recommendations are tailored to them.
